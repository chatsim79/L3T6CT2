Ernie Ball Musicman Axis

This guitar was originally developed by Eddie Van Halen and Musicman guitars. In around 1996 He went to a new company, and Musicman continued to produce the guitar under the moniker "Axis" the a couple of small changes to not run afoul of Copyrights/Trademarks and to actually improve playability for a wider audience.

This is a particularly nice example from 1996, the first year of production in the classic Van Halen Transparent Gold Colour (try googling "EBMM 5150 guitar" for Eddie's personal guitar). The patterning you see on the front of the guitar is not a paint job, nor is it the grain of the wood. It's actually a disease in the wood, which results in a beautiful pattern known as "quilting"
