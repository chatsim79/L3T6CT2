Bacchus Imperial Pro

Deviser, the parent company of Bacchus, are a reasonably small manufacturer that are well known in Japan. They established their name making clones of Fender Stratocasters and Gibson Les Pauls (for more on this see the Fender Stratocaster webpage). This one was a birthday present from my wife, and it's essentially my number one guitar, i.e. its the guitar I pick up 9 times out of 10.

Featuring a one-piece natural oil finished Acaccia body (I suspect it's Australian Blackwood), a maple/rosewood neck/fretboard and Gotoh Hardware throughout, including the Gotoh 1996T Tremolo unit (best on the market in my view) and a tapered bolt-on neck joint for good upper fret access, It's a solid, great sounding instrument which is really easy to pick up and get going with.
