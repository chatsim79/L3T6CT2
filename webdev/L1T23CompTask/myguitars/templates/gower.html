Dan Gower Killer Kali Modified Marshall

That's a mouthful isn't it? So let me break this down.. This amplifier started life as an Marshall JCM800 (google, because that's an entire website in and of itself) built in the UK in 1981. Dan Gower, a lovely chap, then modified the circuitry with his Killer Kali++ package of modifications which increase gain (distortion for metal) , give the EQ controls more range, and other functionality changes. Killer Kali, because the idea of taking stock amplifiers and modifying them most popularly began in California in the late 70's. Dan is one of the more notable builders whose modification actually hold their value.

This amp was a special project for me, and probably the bane of Dan's life for about 6 moths, although he was far too nice to complain. He, on top of the aforementioned package, added an FX loop, various extra tone controls, a clean channel, a footswitch control. It's what would be referred to as fully loaded. Plus we had a custom headshell (enclosure) with some cool vinyl colours added.
